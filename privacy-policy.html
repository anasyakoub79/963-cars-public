Privacy Policy for 963 Cars

Last Updated: September 29, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to 963 Cars ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Profile Information: Address, account type (Personal/Dealer)
  • Listing Information: Car details, descriptions, pricing

2.2 Location Data

  • GPS Location: When you add car listings, we collect location data to display car locations on maps
  • City Information: Your selected city for filtering and search purposes

2.3 Images and Media

  • Car Photos: Images you upload for your car listings
  • Profile Pictures: Optional profile photos (if feature is enabled)

2.4 Usage Data

  • App Activity: Pages viewed, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • View Counts: Number of times your listings are viewed

2.5 Communication Data

  • Contact Interactions: When you contact sellers through the app
  • Notifications: Your notification preferences and history

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Display and manage your car listings
  • Enable communication between buyers and sellers
  • Show your listings on the map
  • Send notifications about your listings
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and abuse

4. Information Sharing

4.1 Public Information

The following information is visible to all app users:

  • Car listings (make, model, price, photos, location on map)
  • Seller's name and phone number (on car detail pages)
  • City location of the car

4.2 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We never sell your personal information to third parties.

4.3 Service Providers

We share data with:

  • Supabase: Our backend database provider (data hosting)
  • Map Services: For displaying car locations
  • Cloud Storage: For storing images

4.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal processes
  • Protect our rights and safety
  • Prevent fraud or security issues

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/SSL)
  • Secure authentication
  • Regular security updates
  • Access controls and monitoring

6. Your Rights (GDPR & Data Protection)

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, contact us at: 963.syria.cars@gmail.com

7. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data retained while account is active
  • Deleted Accounts: Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Sold Listings: Removed from active listings but may be retained for record-keeping

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
