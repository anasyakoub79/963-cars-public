Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

You can create a new account by:

  • Signing up with email and password
  • Signing in with your Google account

How do I post a car for sale?

To post a new listing:

  • Tap the "+" or "Add Listing" button on the home screen
  • Fill in the car details (make, model, year, price, etc.)
  • Add clear photos of the car
  • Set the car location
  • Tap "Publish Listing"

How do I contact a seller?

To contact a car seller:

  • Open the car details page
  • Tap the phone number to call directly
  • Or use the WhatsApp button to message

How do I edit or delete my listing?

To edit or delete your listing:

  • Go to "My Listings" in your account
  • Select the listing you want to modify
  • Tap "Edit" to update details
  • Or tap "Delete" to remove the listing permanently

How do I search for a specific car?

You can search for cars by:

  • Using the search bar on the home page
  • Applying filters (price, year, make, city, etc.)
  • Searching for nearby cars using your location
  • Browsing different categories (new, used, etc.)

How do I add a car to favorites?

Is the app free?

Yes, 963 Cars is completely free to download and use. You can browse cars, post listings, and contact sellers without any fees.

How do I protect my account?

To protect your account:

  • Use a strong and unique password
  • Don't share your account information with anyone
  • Log out after using shared devices
  • Always update the app to the latest version
